About the Project

She-Secure is a mobile-first, clean and distraction-free hub for women’s digital safety in India. Bite-sized Learn modules, quick Action Guides and a searchable Resources directory help users act quickly and safely.

Action Guides — What to do now?

Sextortion
  1. Do not pay or engage further.
  2. Take screenshots & save evidence.
  3. Change passwords & enable 2FA.
  4. Report via platform & cyber cell.
Cyberstalking
  1. Document incidents safely.
  2. Block & tighten privacy.
  3. Disable location sharing.
  4. Escalate to officials with proof.
